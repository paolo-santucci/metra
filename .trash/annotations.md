## The daily card

Tapping a day on the calendar opens the daily card. It's divided into soft sections — you don't have to fill them all in.

## Cycle

The main section lets you mark:

- **Start of flow** — tap to mark the first day
- **End of flow** — Métra calculates this automatically when you stop marking consecutive days
- **Intensity** — light, medium, heavy (optional)

## Mood and energy

Two simple sliders, no numerical scales. Slide right for "higher", left for "lower". There's no judgement: there's no right or wrong value.

## Symptoms

A grid of common options: headache, cramps, bloating, insomnia, and more. Tap to select, tap again to deselect. You can add custom symptoms.

## Free notes

At the bottom of the card there's a space to write anything. A thought, a reflection, a reminder. It's your notebook — use it however you like.
