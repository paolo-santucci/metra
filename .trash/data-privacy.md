## Where your data lives

All Métra data is saved locally on your device, in the app's protected storage. It is never sent to external servers, never passes through the cloud, and is never shared with third parties.

## No account

Métra doesn't require an account to work. We don't collect emails, names, or any other personal data. The app works completely offline.

## Backup

You can export your data at any time as a local file. The backup is a file you can save wherever you prefer — iCloud Drive, a USB stick, or simply on your computer. Métra doesn't manage backups for you: you decide where and when.

## Deleting everything

In settings you'll find the "Delete all data" option. It's immediate and irreversible. Once deleted, the data no longer exists — we don't have copies anywhere.

## No analytics

We don't use analytics, trackers, conversion pixels, or monitoring tools of any kind. We don't know how many people use Métra, how often they open it, or what they note. And that's exactly how we want it.
