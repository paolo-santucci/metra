## Downloading the app

Métra is available on the App Store and Google Play. Search for "Métra" or use the links on the homepage.

The app is free. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads.

## First launch

When you open Métra for the first time, we don't ask you anything: no account, no email, no permissions. The app opens directly to the current month's calendar.

## Your first annotation

Tap a day on the calendar to open the daily card. From here you can:

- Mark the start or end of your cycle
- Note how you feel (mood, energy, symptoms)
- Add a free-form note

You don't have to fill in everything. Note only what you feel like, when you feel like it.

## Navigating the calendar

Swipe left and right to change months. Annotated days show a coloured dot — the colour depends on the cycle phase, but you can customise it in settings.
